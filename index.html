Welcome to the India MedTech Expo 2025

India: Global MedTech Manufacturing Hub Precision Engineering yet Affordable

Country’s biggest ever medical device event showcasing India’s MedTech ecosystem. Opportunities to network and explore collaborations. Baseline demand and growth for Vision 2047. Presentations by Union & State Procurement Agencies for the next 5 years. Accelerate investment into MedTech sector by both global & domestic companies. Development of ecosystem – Supplier, Infrastructure, Manufacturing, R&D, Start-up, Academia and Skilling. 300+ foreign buyers from 50+ countries.

Bharat Health 2025 is a mega initiative of Government of India. This prestigious event is being organized by the Department of Commerce, Government of India, in association with Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Ministry of Ayush, Export Promotion Council for Medical Device (EPCMD), Pharmexcil, EEPC, SEPC and Ayushexcil. Each Export Promotion Council will organise its lead sector program under the aegis of Bharat Health ie. India MedTech Expo by EPCMD, IPHEX by Pharmexcil, India Heals by SEPC and Arogya by Ayushexcil.

After the resounding success of the 1st edition, Department of Pharmaceuticals & Export Promotion Council for Medical Device (EPCMD) will be organising the 2nd edition of India MedTech Expo 2025. Like in past, this edition too, will be supported by all Medical Devices sectoral industry associations such as AIMED, ADMI, MTAI, AMTZ, AMCHAM, APACMED, ADVAMED, APMEI, ASSOCHAM, CII, FICCI, INVEST INDIA, PHD, USIBC, NATHEALTH, USISPF, etc.
